Teaching Interests

As a graduate instructor, I teach a mixture of courses in digital media studies, film studies, and composition. My focus in these classes is often two-fold: first, to help my students encounter a broad range of interesting material in disciplines that they often donâ€™t known exist, let alone that you can make a career out of engaging; and second, to teach my students how to weaponize their curiosity, such that they can learn how to follow hunches, develop sophisticated arguments, and understand the humanities as a function of their own lived experiences. I also run the occasional workshop targeted at my fellow graduate students, on topics such as basic web development, social media, and digital humanities methods.
